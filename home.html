Try clicking on something. The site evolves with the traffic it sees and optimizes itself to direct every user to one thing (which the designers deem worthy). In this demo I'm trying to make you click on a specific bit of text. The site adapts over time to persuade you to click on only a single bit of text. I expect it to lead to some interesting things.
